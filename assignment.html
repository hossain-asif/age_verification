Child

A child (plural: children) is a human being between the stages of birth and puberty or between the developmental period of infancy and puberty.

Adolescence

The transitional period between childhood and adulthood is known as adolescence. The individuals of this age group are known as adolescents or teenagers.

Adult

An adult is a human that has reached full growth.In the human context, the term adult has meanings associated with social and legal concepts.
